About Me

Deadbird Warranty

Inspired by my time in retail at an outdoor gear brand, and most products had a limited lifetime warranty. Limited warranty sounds amazing, until customers had to file them. The system for filing a warranty claim did not allow customers to check on the status of the claim, or if they decided that they no longer wanted the claim they could delete it. This led calls to the shop of customers asking us, how long will this take, is there any update, which all we could do was apologize to the customer that we didn't have any update and told them they just had to wait. Sad face , it sucks to keep apologizing.

In this app, I tackled just that, created an app where customers can file a warranty, where they will be given a warranty number, which they can use to look up the status or if they wish to no longer go on with the claim, they can also delete the claim.
